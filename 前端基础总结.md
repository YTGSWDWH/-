## Element Ui
### 图片占满父容器：:fit="fit"
### el-col中的元素居中：display：flex；justify-centent: center; align-items: center;
### 先按行显示图像，在按列显示图像：
```vue
 <el-row :gutter="10">
      <el-col :span="4" v-for="(n,i) in list" :key="i">
        <img :src="`${n.purl}`" alt="" height="200px" width="100%">
      </el-col>
  </el-row>
```
